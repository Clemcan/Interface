About the project, about the site:

This project has been realized by a team of students, PhD students, and professor from three different universities, in Sweden and in France. it aims towards enabling everyone to have access to multiple projects in the Software Development field, and to have the possibility to store their own projects in it.

The front-end was realised in Sweden by a French student from Esiee Amiens, over the course of his three months internship inside the Software Engineering Department at Chalmers University of Technology.
The back-end was realised in France, by French student, at IRCICA Lille.

The end result shows the cooperation between Chalmers University of Technology, Esiee Amiens and IRCICA Lille.
